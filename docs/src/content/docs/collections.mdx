---
title: Collections
description: Organize related rules into folders.
---

Collections keep rules grouped by app, workflow, test account, customer type, or project.

## Built-In Collections

**All Rules** shows active rules across every collection. **Default** stores rules that are not assigned to a custom collection.

## Manage Collections

Create collections from the sidebar, move rules between collections, export a collection when you need a focused backup, and delete collections when the whole group is no longer useful.

## Drag And Drop

Use drag and drop to reorder rules and move them into the collection that matches the workflow.
